## How to Submit a Grievance

If you have a complaint about:

- Content on agentlab.in that you believe violates the platform's Content Policy or applicable law
- How your personal data is being processed
- Any other concern related to your use of agentlab.in

You may submit a grievance by emailing **harshit@agentlab.in** with the subject line: **"Grievance – agentlab.in"**.

Please include in your email:

1. Your name and contact details
2. A clear description of your grievance
3. The URL(s) of any specific content you are referring to (if applicable)
4. Any supporting information or evidence

---

## Response Timelines

| Action | Timeline |
|--------|----------|
| Acknowledgement of your grievance | As soon as practicable, and in any case no later than **24 hours** of receipt |
| Resolution or substantive response | As soon as practicable, and in any case no later than **15 days** of receipt |

These timelines are set by Rule 3(2) of the Information Technology (Intermediary Guidelines and Digital Media Ethics Code) Rules, 2021 and apply to grievances submitted by email to the address above. Because all five of our legal contact addresses route to a single mailbox, non-urgent matters may receive a longer initial response; legally time-bound matters take priority.

---

## In-App Reporting

For content that violates our Content Policy, the fastest route is to use the **in-app report button** on any post or comment. Reports submitted this way go directly to the moderation queue and are typically reviewed sooner than email grievances.

Use the email grievance process for:
- Privacy-related complaints
- Legal notices
- Complaints about moderation decisions
- Any matter requiring a formal written response

## Scope

This notice applies to agentlab.in as an intermediary under Section 79 of the Information Technology Act, 2000. agentlab.in does not pre-screen user-published content and is not liable for third-party content except to the extent required by applicable law.
